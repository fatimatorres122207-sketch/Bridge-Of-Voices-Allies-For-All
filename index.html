About

Bridge of Voices: Allies is an inclusive student-led organization. We are just starting out — this site is currently a planning hub with our ideas and upcoming initiatives.

Event Checklist

  • Cultural Heritage Fair — booths, food, music, dance, and stories from different communities. Guest speakers + local activists.
  • Storytelling Nights — students share experiences of identity, struggle, or resilience.
  • Movie + Discussion Nights — films/documentaries highlighting minority voices + guided conversations.
  • Allyship Training Workshops — standing up against discrimination + knowing your rights (ICE & Police).
  • Art Sessions — poetry, photography, or any art exploring culture, struggle, or self-expression.
  • Open Mic Night — music, spoken word, dance, and performances celebrating diversity.
