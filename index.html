About Us

We are an organzation that supports underrepresented and minority students through education, community events, and peer mentorship. Our goal is to make school a safer, more inclusive place for everyone.

Allyship Workshop

Interactive training on how to support peers, intervene safely, and create inclusive spaces.

Storytelling Night

Open mic where students share their lived experiences. Safe space & moderated discussion.

Resource Drive

Collecting clothing, hygiene items, and culturally relevant food for local community centers.
