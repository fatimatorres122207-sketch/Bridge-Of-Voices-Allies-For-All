Our Logo

By Toney | November 7, 2025

I wanted to be a part of Bridge Of Voices because I care deeply about equality within every race, gender, sexuality, etc. I want people to be comfortable in their own skin, and comfortable being around people who are different from them. These issues have and will always be top tier issues for me, because seeing someone being treated differently for something they can't change nor asked for, provokes me. As a person who is mixed with Native American, Mexican and African American I’ve noticed separation between all underrepresented groups. Noticing this makes me want to encourage more people to uplift and understand others for where they come from.

For the logo of Bridge Of Voices I wanted to have hands coming together to make a heart, to show the support and encourage more people to create a supportive community. I specifically wanted the hands to be different shades to represent all black and brown people and to make them feel heard. I was inspired by the downtown mural in Cedar Rapids, Iowa “Together We Bloom”. The mural is a wave-like chane of hands that are different shades of brown, that is built and stable on the support of each hand. I would like to create an environment that builds on the support of each person.
